What is a Capstone?

A short introduction about the high school capstone project

A Capstone is a project completed in your spare time that demonstrates a student's learning by pursuing something in a subject field they are passiontate about and/or expect to continue past their secondary education

A Capstone Timeline

1

Creating a Proposal

Creating an outline for your project, including a timeline, the product, and what core competencies will be used during the project.

2

Acquiring a Mentor

In creating a capstone project, you need a mentor that can provide guidance and knowledge during the R&D phase.

3

Research and Development

Researching about your topic and developing the research that will culminate into the final product.

4

The Final Product

Your project should have an end result, which could be in the form of a type of media, a physical object, or anything in-between.

5

Presentation to Your Peers

You then present your project to your peers and teachers, showing the progress and eventual end result of the capstone project.
