I value our planet the most, since if nobody cares about it, it would not care for us. My family always tries to lower our consumption of carbon dioxide emmiting sources, as well as donating to environmental organizations and purchasing items that are carbon neutral. My dad also works as a green sustainability manager.

Family is one value I can't live without them. It's as simple as that. We have a simple relationship and stick to it. We vent to each other when we're having a tough day in the outside world, and we can relax around while not caring about social standards.

Since I value Earth, i love adventuring and seeing different sights. Whether it's in populated metropolises, or in the middle of nowhere, there is always something to see that will intrigue me. Animals

During the summer of 2016, I became vegetarien for a couple of reasons. One, I despise animal abuse, and secondly, I didn't really like the taste of meat, other than bacon and breakfast sausages. Ever since then, I've been vegetarien, and other than jokes from my friends and a bit more time taken to decide what to eat for dinner, I don't miss it at all. I most likely will continue being vegetarien unless it's absolutey necessary. I also like piglets.
