The Testing- Movie Poster

This is my reflection on a movie poster we created for English 9.

The Testing Trilogy surrounds itself by survival and that’s why I chose the person who acted Laura Croft in Tomb Raider. I think it was a strength in my movie poster. I could improve on the image that I put on the poster, but I think it’s very simple to understand.

Using the movie poster creator (postermywall.com) was very simple and easy to understand, and it couldn’t of been easier to create a simple and attracting movie poster. Although I wish that it had more customization. 

It was an interesting project and next time I think I could do even better.
